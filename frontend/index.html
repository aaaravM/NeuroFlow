About Us

We’re Vidhyuth K, Pradyun J, and Aarav M. NeuroFlow started as a simple idea: if stress and focus are a daily part of school life, why not build a tool that makes them easier to understand and manage?

As high school students, we juggle classes, clubs, projects, and exams. We know the feeling of staring at a screen, trying to focus, and not knowing what’s holding us back. That’s why NeuroFlow is designed to be relatable and practical—no complicated dashboards, just a clean experience that helps you stay in the zone and shows you what to try next.

For others—students, self‑learners, or professionals—the same principles apply. Short, consistent feedback loops lead to better habits. Our approach focuses on privacy‑friendly, on‑device capture during your study session, then gives straightforward guidance you can act on immediately.
